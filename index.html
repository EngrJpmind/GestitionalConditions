The Purpose of this website

Providing information about bleeding during pregnancy is an essential part of our website's purpose. It is crucial for expecting mothers to understand the different types of bleeding that can occur during pregnancy and when to seek medical attention. By providing accurate and reliable information, we aim to educate and empower pregnant women to make informed decisions about their health and the health of their baby.
